About

Got Chips LLC is a small, engineering‑focused machine shop built around curiosity, precision, and a genuine love for making things. We help people turn ideas into real, functional parts — whether it’s a one‑off prototype or a small production run.

Who We Are

We’re a hands‑on shop that believes in clear communication, practical engineering, and doing things the right way. When you work with us, you talk directly to the person machining your parts — no layers, no confusion, no surprises.

Why We Started

Got Chips LLC was created to make high‑quality machining accessible and friendly. Too many shops feel intimidating or hard to approach. We wanted to build something different — a place where people feel comfortable asking questions, exploring ideas, and building cool things.

How We Work

  • We communicate clearly and honestly
  • We focus on practical, real‑world solutions
  • We iterate quickly and efficiently
  • We care about the details that matter

If you’re looking for a shop that’s easy to work with and serious about quality, you’re in the right place.
