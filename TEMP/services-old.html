Services

We help turn ideas into real, functional parts. Whether you’re early in development or ready for production, we offer practical, engineering‑driven support at every step.

CNC Machining

High‑quality machining for prototypes, functional parts, and small production runs. Clean finishes, tight tolerances, and reliable results.

Prototype Development

Still refining your design? No problem. We help you iterate quickly, catch issues early, and get to a working part faster.

DFM (Design for Manufacturability)

We look at your design from a machinist’s perspective and help simplify, strengthen, and reduce cost without compromising function.

Reverse Engineering

Have a part but no drawing? We can recreate it, improve it, or adapt it for modern machining processes.

Fixture + Tooling Design

Custom fixtures and modular setups designed for real‑world shop use. Practical, efficient, and built to make machining easier.

Small‑Batch Production

When you need more than a prototype but not a full production run, we can bridge the gap with consistent, repeatable machining.
